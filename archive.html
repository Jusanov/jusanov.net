Retirement of the Jusanov Alias

After thinking for a while, I have finally come to the decision of retiring the Jusanov alias. This name was originally made up by my friend Brendan and I over a year ago in a Skype conversation. All we thought of the name is that it was better than the one I had been using previously.

After thinking about what the future will bring me, I have decided to lessen the amount of video games I play, the video game related content I create, and the use of the Jusanov name. In accordance with this, I have refactored my GitHub account name to be a personal account and have updated all other programming services accordingly.

I always say to my friends and family that I can't predict the future, but I feel like this will be a step in the direction of my future.

- Jusanov (Justin)
